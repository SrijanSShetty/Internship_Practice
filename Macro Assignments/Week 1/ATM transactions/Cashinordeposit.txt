To deposit money using ATM
Step 1: Start
Step 2: Visit the ATM
Step 3: Cashin your ATM card
Step 4: Click on the deposit option from the given set of options if ATM allow the operation
Step 5: Enter your pin
Step 6: Enter the amount to deposit and the notes allowed for deposit
Step 7: Enter the number of notes with respect to 100,200,500 and 2000 denominators with respect to the machines
Step 8: Enter the amount and arrange the cash as directed for deposit at the ATM
Step 9: Recollect the rejected notes and replace with suitable one's like dry and crisp to avoid any delays at ATM's
Step 10: Cash in the desired amount and receive confirmation receipt
