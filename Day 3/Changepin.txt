To change pin of transactions at ATM
Step 1: Start
Step 2: Gear up to ATM
Step 3: Handout your card to the ATM machine
Step 4: Enter the pin
Step 5: If entered pin is crct //Nowadays choose your transaction pin are user dependent for the first time 
        goto step 6
        else
        Error got step 4
Step 6: Click on change pin option
Step 7: Enter your mobile number and confirm with One Time Password 
Step 8: Once mobile verification is done Enter email id for verification and also confirm with One Time Password
Step 9: Enter your new pin 
Step 10: Confirm your new pin
Step 11: Stop
